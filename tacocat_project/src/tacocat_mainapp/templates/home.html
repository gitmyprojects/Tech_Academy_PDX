The Rules

The rules are simple. Once you are signed up, choose your battle; Bacon vs Sausage, Chips Ahoy vs Oreo, etc. Then choose your side. Read the description of the item you will be arguing for. The description must be the base of your argument. For example, if you are defending vanilla ice cream, you can't use "you can add mint and chocolate chips to it." as an argument because you are changing the item itself. Don't over-think it! This is meant to be fun! That being said, leave it to your opponent and voters to decide if your argument is venturing too far away from the actual item being defended.
